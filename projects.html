Enchantability

Enchantability is a mod which allows you to enchant yourself with unique abilities by placing enchanted books into your Ender Chest

Each enchanted book has been given an ability which is tailored to how the enchant behaves but specifically made unique as to not make the original enchantment obsolete

Rebirth of the Mobs

Rebirth of the Mobs allows you to write configs to customise additional behaviour in mobs.

The behaviours range from adding immunities to mobs to spawning monsters when an entity is below certain health and allowing mobs to regen health whenever they slain their enemies

CurseForge Github

Pick Pocketer

Pick Pocketer allows players to steal from their enemies!

You can steal from mobs, for loot is either generated from the mobs loot table or customised in configs, and players, which is limited to single item per steal attempt and the victim has a chance of being notified of it!
